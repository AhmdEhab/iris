About the Project

IRIS is an easy-to-use learning platform made for children with intellectual disabilities. It helps them learn through fun activities, simple lessons, and interactive games. The platform also supports families by giving guidance and tools to understand their child’s progress. IRIS uses smart technology to adjust the learning experience for each child, making it suitable for different abilities and learning speeds. The goal of IRIS is to help every child learn, grow, and feel confident — because every child deserves a chance to shine.
